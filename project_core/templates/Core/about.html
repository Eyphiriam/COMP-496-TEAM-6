About This Application

CT-NeuroNet is an innovative tool designed by a dedicated team of researchers and developers. It harnesses cutting-edge convolutional neural networks to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in quickly analyzing CT scans for potential COVID-19 indicators. Our mission is to make advanced medical imaging analysis more accessible and reliable.

This platform is part of ongoing research. We value your feedback and contributions as we work towards improving diagnostic efficiency and patient outcomes.

About CT-NeuroNet

CT-NeuroNet leverages AI to analyze CT scans for COVID-19 detection, offering insights to healthcare providers and patients.
